The Analyst Behind
the Numbers

I'm a finance professional based in Jaipur, Rajasthan, focused on building a rigorous foundation in equity research and investment analysis. My work is grounded in first-principles thinking — understanding a business before modelling it.

I've cleared CFA Levels I and II and am registered for the Level III exam in August 2026. I've also cleared CA Intermediate, which has given me strong command of financial accounting, taxation, and corporate law — tools that matter when you're reading an annual report, not just a summary.

My research approach combines fundamental analysis, detailed financial statement deep-dives, and DCF/comparables-based valuation. I'm interested in both large-cap coverage and identifying mispriced small/mid-cap opportunities in Indian equities.

This site is a living repository of my research output. Reports, theses, and industry work will be published here as they're completed.
